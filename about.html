About MRB

A Stockholm, Sweden UEFN studio built for creator-led Fortnite launches.

MRB exists to turn playable ideas into polished worlds, systems, and launch moments that creators can explain, film, and promote with confidence. Based in Stockholm, Sweden, MRB has worked with UEFN since the day it launched.

Studio mission

Games built as launch moments.

Philosophy

The best creator game experiences are clear before they are complicated.

MRB focuses on strong hooks, polished presentation, practical production, and audience-readable design. The goal is not to make every feature possible. The goal is to make the right moment feel sharp.

Values

How MRB approaches the work.

Premium does not mean vague. It means intentional, edited, stable, and ready for public attention.

Clarity

Every project needs a hook that creators and audiences can understand quickly.

Polish

Presentation, usability, pacing, and feel matter because they shape trust immediately.

Proof

Case studies, screenshots, clips, and learnings show how decisions were made.

Speed

MRB works with speed: fast decisions, fast playable checks, and a production rhythm that keeps launches moving.

Platforms

Focused on UEFN for creator-led Fortnite launches.

MRB currently supports UEFN / Fortnite projects only, so the studio can stay focused on the platform, production constraints, and creator workflows that matter most.
