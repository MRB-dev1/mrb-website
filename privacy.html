1. Who we are

MRB is a creator-focused game development studio offering custom development services, digital products, and partnership inquiries through this website.

2. Information we collect

We may collect information you submit through contact forms, booking forms, email, product requests, or support messages. This can include your name, email address, role, platform, project details, budget range, timeline, and message content.

We may also collect basic technical information such as browser type, device type, referring pages, approximate location, and website usage data if analytics are enabled.

3. How we use information

We use information to respond to inquiries, schedule calls, scope projects, provide support, deliver digital products, improve the website, protect the site from abuse, and keep business records.

4. Payments and third-party tools

Payments, downloads, analytics, calendar bookings, email, and forms may be handled by third-party providers such as Lemon Squeezy, Stripe, Calendly, Google Analytics, Plausible, Vercel, or email providers. These services process data under their own policies.

5. Cookies and analytics

The website may use cookies or similar technologies for analytics, security, preferences, and checkout or booking flows. If required by law, cookie consent should be configured before launch.

If analytics are enabled, MRB asks visitors to accept or reject analytics cookies before Google Analytics loads. Visitors can reopen Cookie settings and change that choice later.

6. Data retention

We keep information only as long as reasonably needed for communication, support, business records, legal compliance, and legitimate studio operations.

7. Data security

MRB should collect only the information needed to operate the studio, respond to inquiries, provide products or services, and meet business obligations. Reasonable administrative, technical, and organizational safeguards should be used to protect information.

8. Privacy rights

Depending on where you live, you may have rights to know what personal information is collected, request access, request correction or deletion, opt out of certain sharing, or receive information about how personal information is used. MRB should provide any required regional notices before launch.

You may request access, correction, deletion, or restriction of personal information where applicable by contacting MRB. Some records may need to be retained for legal, tax, security, or dispute purposes.

9. Contact

For privacy questions, contact privacy@mrb.ink.
